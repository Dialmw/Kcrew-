About K Crew

K Crew Dancers is a passionate dance collective based in Kibabii University, mastering the art of blue dance vibes and hype choreography that gets everyone moving! Since way back, we've been igniting stages and building community through our electrifying performances with signature blue energy. Your contributions fuel our dream to compete, create, and inspire the next generation of dancers! 💙✨

  1. Open M-Pesa on your phone (SIM Toolkit, USSD *334#, or M-Pesa App) 📱
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa ➡️ Pay Bill
  3. Enter Business Number: 567890 (K Crew Dancers)
  4. Enter Account Number:
  5. Enter Amount: KES
  6. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and confirm ✅
  7. Wait for SMS confirmation from M-Pesa 📩
