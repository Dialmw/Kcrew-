About K Crew Dancers

Welcome to K Crew Dancers, Kibabii University's premier dance collective! We're a passionate group of talented dancers specializing in urban dance energy - our signature fusion of hip-hop, contemporary, and Afrobeat styles that electrifies audiences across Kenya.

Founded with the mission to spread positive energy through movement, we've performed at over 100+ events, won 8 major competitions, and built a community of 15+ dedicated dancers who live and breathe the art of dance.
